|\cdots | 中间三点 |
|\infty| 无穷大符号 |

下面这个公式表示multinomial概率分布:  

$$ P\left(X_{1}=x_{1}, \ldots, X_{k}=x_{k}\right)=\frac{n !}{x_{1} ! \cdots x_{k} !} p_{1}^{x_{1}} \cdots p_{k}^{x_{k}} $$
